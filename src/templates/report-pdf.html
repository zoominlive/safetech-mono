3.0 ASSESSMENT METHODOLOGY

Assessment Approach:

The assessment was conducted in accordance with applicable regulations and industry best practices, including:

  • Visual inspection of accessible areas
  • Review of building plans and historical documentation
  • Sampling and laboratory analysis where required
  • Documentation of findings with photographic evidence
  • Area-specific assessment of designated substances

Areas Assessed:

{{areasAssessed}}

Assessment Scope:

The assessment included evaluation of the following materials and conditions:

  • Asbestos-containing materials (ACM)
  • Lead-containing materials
  • Silica-containing materials
  • Mercury-containing equipment
  • PCB-containing equipment
  • Mold growth and water damage
  • Pest infestation and droppings
  • Other hazardous materials
